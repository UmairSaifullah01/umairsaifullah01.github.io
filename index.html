Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer? expand_more
I offer product design, web development, UI/UX audits, and consulting services to help bring your digital products to life.
What is your design process? expand_more
My design process follows a user-centered approach: discovery and research, ideation and wireframing, design and prototyping, user testing, and iterative refinement. I collaborate closely with clients throughout each phase.
How do you handle project timelines? expand_more
I provide clear timelines at project kickoff and maintain regular communication about progress. Most projects range from 2-8 weeks depending on scope. I always prioritize quality while respecting deadlines.
Can you work with existing teams? expand_more
Absolutely! I'm experienced in collaborating with in-house teams, remote teams, and cross-functional groups. I adapt my workflow to integrate seamlessly with your existing processes and tools.
What tools do you use? expand_more
I use industry-standard tools including Figma for design, Framer for prototyping, React for development, and various collaboration tools like Slack, Notion, and GitHub. I'm flexible and can work with your preferred stack.
